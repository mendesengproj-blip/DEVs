DGSAT-I extended-source slip prediction (Paper III)
============================================================
M = 3.000e+08 Msun, r_eff = r_p = 4.70 kpc
r_MOND = 0.590 kpc, epsilon = r_eff/r_MOND = 7.96

Point-source (Paper I):
  g_N(r_eff) = 1.893e-12 m/s^2,   y = 0.0158
  eta - 1    = 3.95 %

Extended (Plummer enclosed mass at r_eff):
  g_N(r_eff) = 6.693e-13 m/s^2,  y = 0.0056
  eta - 1    = 6.68 %

Ratio (extended / point) = 1.690

CAVEAT (Paper III, sec. II): the operator-level analysis of
Task 1 shows the slip field is generated by a non-local
operator whose Green function decays as r^{-1/2}.  The local-g_N
substitution above is therefore an UPPER bound on the
conservative reduction; the full non-local convolution would
yield a value between the point-source eta-1 and the local-g_N
eta-1 reported here.
